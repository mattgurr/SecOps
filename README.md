# Cybersecurity Operations Mentoring Program

A structured mentoring program designed to help people move into (or deeper into) cybersecurity operations over 90 days. Whether you're finishing a degree, switching careers, or already working in IT, this repo gives you a tailored plan, study guide, and lab structure to make real progress.

---

## Why This Exists

Breaking into cybersecurity operations is hard, not because the content doesn't exist, but because there's too much of it, none of it is personalised, and most people don't know where to start.

This program cuts through that by:

- Grouping mentees into realistic cohorts based on where they actually are.
- Giving each cohort a tailored 30‑60‑90 day plan, study guide, and lab structure.
- Focusing on **artifacts**: small, real outputs (labs, writeups, projects) that become your portfolio and interview stories.
- Keeping things honest about what entry‑level cybersecurity roles actually look like day‑to‑day.

---

## Who This Is For

We group mentees into three cohorts:

| Cohort | Who you are | Starting focus |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **Cohort 1 – IT Students & Grads** | Studying or recently finished an IT‑related course | Build foundations, labs, and a first portfolio |
| **Cohort 2 – Career Changers** | Experience in another field, moving into security | Map transferable skills, pick a path, build artifacts |
| **Cohort 3 – IT Professionals** | Already working in IT (helpdesk, dev, cloud, etc.) | Add security to your current role, then transition |

Not sure which cohort fits? Read `/shared/01-career-paths-overview-all-cohorts-short.md` for a quick "choose your lane" guide.
