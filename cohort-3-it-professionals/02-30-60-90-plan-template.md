# 02 - Cohort 3 - 30-60-90 Day Plan Template - IT Professionals

## Overview
Use this template to pivot from your current IT role into more security-focused work, ideally leveraging your current environment first.

- 30 days: Observe and align
- 60 days: Contribute and specialize
- 90 days: Own and transition
