# Cybersecurity Operations Mentoring

Welcome! This repo supports a mentoring program focused on helping you move into (or deeper into) cybersecurity operations over 90 days.

It’s organised around three cohorts, each with tailored questions, study plans, and lab guides.

---

## Who This Is For

We group mentees into three broad cohorts:

1. **Cohort 1 – IT Students & Grads**  
   You’re studying (or recently finished) an IT‑related course and want your first security role.

2. **Cohort 2 – Career Changers**  
   You have experience in another field and want to transition into cybersecurity.

3. **Cohort 3 – Existing IT Professionals**  
   You already work in IT (helpdesk, sysadmin, dev, cloud, etc.) and want to move into a more security‑focused role.

Each cohort has:

- Intake questions to understand your starting point and goals  
- A 30‑60‑90 day plan template  
- A focused study and lab guide  
- A one‑page quick‑start PDF

---

## How the Mentoring Program Works

The mentoring program is built around three simple ideas:

- **Clarity:** Choose a realistic target direction (e.g., SOC / IR, GRC, cloud security, DFIR).
- **Consistency:** Make steady weekly progress (study + labs), not random bursts.
- **Artifacts:** Produce small, concrete outputs (labs, projects, writeups) that become your portfolio.

We use the same 30‑60‑90 structure across cohorts:

- **Days 1–30:** Explore, map where you are now, and set direction.  
- **Days 31–60:** Build skills and ship small projects.  
- **Days 61–90:** Consolidate, create interview‑ready artifacts, and start applying or transitioning.

---
How to Get the Most From Mentoring
Show up prepared: Bring your answers to the intake questions and any existing CV/LinkedIn/GitHub.

Be honest about constraints: Time, family, money, current job realities – we’ll design around them.

Document everything: Notes, lab writeups, and small projects become your portfolio and interview stories.

Ask questions early: If you’re stuck on what to do next, say so – that’s what the program is for.
