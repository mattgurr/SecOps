# Cohort 1 - Intake Questions

Where are you now in your studies: degree, diploma, bootcamp, or self‑taught, and how far through are you (year/term)?

Which cybersecurity operations roles currently sound most interesting to you: SOC analyst, incident responder, DFIR, threat intel, cloud security, or something else?

What have you done so far outside formal classes: labs, CTFs, home lab, student SOC, open‑source projects, or security clubs?

In the next 12–18 months, what would feel like a clear win for you: first security internship, part‑time SOC role, graduate program offer, or a strong project portfolio?

How many hours per week can you realistically commit to security‑focused practice (labs, projects, reading) on top of your study load?
