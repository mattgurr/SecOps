# 01 - Cohort 2 - Career Changers - Intake Questions 

# Questions tuned for non-traditional backgrounds

What is your current or most recent profession, and which parts of that work do you most enjoy (problem‑solving, people, process, tech, writing, etc.)?

Which cybersecurity areas are you most drawn to right now: SOC / IR, GRC / compliance, red team, cloud security, DFIR, or security engineering?

What have you already tried toward this transition: online courses, labs, certs, CTFs, networking, or informational interviews?

Looking 12–18 months ahead, what outcome would you define as success: first role in security (any level), a role in a specific niche, or validating whether this path is really for you?

How much time and budget can you dedicate each week/month to learning, labs, and certifications over the next 6–12 months?

You can then map their transferable skills and propose realistic entry points and certification paths.
